How It Works

Three steps from application to acceptance.

A calm, deliberate process — because rushing an application is how good candidates end up with generic ones.

STEP 01

You apply

Fill in a short questionnaire about your background, target programs, and what you need. It takes a few minutes and tells me whether I’m the right fit for you.

STEP 02

I map the strategy

If there’s a seat and you’re a fit, I walk you through your profile, shortlist programs, and agree on exactly what to write and in what order.

STEP 03

I draft & refine

I write, you respond, I revise — until every document is sharp, honest, and unmistakably yours. Then you submit with confidence.
